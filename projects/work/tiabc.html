Time is a Black Circle — Dawn Ng

As part of the Design Team, I assisted Dawn in bringing the vision of this circular time portal to life.

Time is a Black Circle was the key installation of the Singapore Night Festival 2023, organised by the National Heritage Board. Held in the National Museum, the work is a cylindrical roller disco capturing the ephemerality of the 60s and 70s.

I worked closely with Dawn, the architecture team, and Singapore Night Festival to produce the gradient wall, as well as create the key visuals for the installation.

The key visual aimed to capture the visual language of the 60s and 70s through vinyl records.

Experimentation with a balance of “carelessness” in typography observed in references.

The gradient wall was conjured to evoke the hues of the 60s and 70s. Starting off with colours picked off a series of images curated by Dawn. A series of gradients were created, each increasing in complexity and nuance.
