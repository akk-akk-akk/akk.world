In-House Design, Marketing, Socials / 2019

Morning

As the first graphic designer at the startup, I worked off existing brand assets to create digital and physical collaterals that established the startup's visibility and tone.

At its inception, MORNING aimed to establish the Morning Marketplace, a platform that curated coffee capsules from leading roasters around the world.

I was tasked to bring out certain characteristics that represented the brand, such as accessibility, convenience, all at the same time, maintaining a certain exclusivity that Morning would like to bring forth to their customers through its visual identity and marketing.

Brand identity and assets (font, coffee pod icons, etc.) courtesy of ACRE.
