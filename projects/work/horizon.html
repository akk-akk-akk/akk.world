Spatial, Exhibition, Publication / 2023

Horizon — Liu Liling

Wordmark, key visual and exhibition booklet for Liling's solo exhibition at [hearth], Gillman Barracks.

Wordmark and Key Visual

Liling's series of works for Horizon comprise a series of inkjet prints that draw upon observations of the sky. The key visual is an artwork, overlaid with the title text lightly segmented by the refraction of the horizon.

Exhibition Booklet

I wanted to tap intothe headspace of Liling's medium-based experiments (acrylic, lightboxes, etc.), and have the publication serve as an extension of the works.

Organic Grid

Liling's works embrace the visual nuances produced as a result of the printing process.

Instead of strict grid and margin proportioning, we let the cover artwork reveal organic markers for fold lines, margins and grids.

This resulted in a publication that, when closed, leaves an off-centered gap that held space for a pale streak in the artwork.
